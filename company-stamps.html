● Round Company Stamp

The classic round company seal

The round stamp is the signature of UAE business. Your company name arcs along the top, your P.O. Box and emirate along the bottom, and your logo or initials sit dead-centre — the layout banks, courts and clients instantly recognise as "official".

Built on a 38 mm self-inking mechanism, it delivers a perfectly circular, balanced impression every single time. The die is laser-engraved for hairline-sharp text, so even the smallest P.O. Box digits stay crisp after thousands of presses.

⬭ Oval Company Stamp

An elegant oval for longer names

When your company name is long — or you want it in both Arabic and English — the oval gives you the horizontal room the round simply can't. It keeps the refined, official feel of a seal while comfortably fitting more text on every line.

The elongated die is a favourite for trade-license seals and corporate documents in Dubai, where bilingual layouts are the norm. Logo in the middle, company name above, P.O. Box and emirate below — beautifully balanced across the wider footprint.

▭ Rectangle Company Stamp

The information workhorse

When you need to stamp full details — company name, address, TRN, phone and email — the rectangle gives you straight, generous lines to fit it all. It's the stamp your accounts and admin teams will reach for every day.

Because the layout is linear rather than curved, text stays large and easy to read, making it ideal for approvals, "RECEIVED" and "PAID" marks, stock control and any document where legibility matters more than ceremony.

Good to know

Company stamp questions, answered

What is a self-inking company stamp?

A self-inking stamp has the ink pad built into the body. Each time you press, the die flips onto the pad and re-inks itself automatically — giving you hundreds of clean, consistent impressions before the pad needs a quick refill. No separate ink pad, no mess.

Which company stamp shape should I choose?

Round and oval are the most common for official company seals and trade-license use in the UAE. Choose rectangle when you need to fit a full address, TRN and contact details, and square for a modern, logo-centred look. You can preview any shape in our online designer before you decide.

How fast can I get a company stamp in Dubai?

Most company stamps are produced the same day. At checkout you can pick next-day delivery, 4-hour express courier via Careem Box, or pickup in Deira, Dubai — we'll message you on WhatsApp the moment it's ready.

Can I add my logo and Arabic text?

Yes. Our designer supports a centred logo upload plus bilingual Arabic and English text. After you order, our team refines the final artwork — spacing, alignment and proportions — so the impression looks flawless.
