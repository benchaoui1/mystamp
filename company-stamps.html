The basics

What exactly is a
company stamp?

A company stamp — also called a company seal — places your approved business details on everyday documents quickly and consistently. In the UAE it commonly includes your registered trade name in English and Arabic, P.O. Box, emirate and, when needed, your logo or trade-licence number.

A sharp, consistent impression makes invoices, contracts, bank forms and company correspondence easier to identify and process. That is why the layout and die quality of your company stamp in the UAE matter.

The classic round company seal

The round stamp is a familiar UAE business layout. Your company name arcs along the top, your P.O. Box and emirate sit along the bottom, and your logo or initials stay centred for a balanced, professional impression.

Built on a 38 mm self-inking mechanism, it delivers a perfectly circular, balanced impression every single time. The die is laser-engraved for hairline-sharp text, so even the smallest P.O. Box digits stay crisp after thousands of presses.

⬭ Oval Company Stamp

An elegant oval for longer names

When your company name is long — or you want it in both Arabic and English — the oval gives you the horizontal room the round simply can't. It keeps the refined, official feel of a seal while comfortably fitting more text on every line.

The elongated die is a favourite for trade-license seals and corporate documents in Dubai, where bilingual layouts are the norm. Logo in the middle, company name above, P.O. Box and emirate below — beautifully balanced across the wider footprint.

▭ Rectangle Company Stamp

The information workhorse

When you need to stamp full details — company name, address, TRN, phone and email — the rectangle gives you straight, generous lines to fit it all. It's the stamp your accounts and admin teams will reach for every day.

Because the layout is linear rather than curved, text stays large and easy to read, making it ideal for approvals, "RECEIVED" and "PAID" marks, stock control and any document where legibility matters more than ceremony.

A modern, logo-first statement

The square stamp is the contemporary alternative to the round seal. Its equal sides create a naturally balanced canvas — perfect for putting a bold, centred logo front and centre, framed by your company details.

Design-led businesses, studios and modern brands love the square for the way it makes a logo the hero. You still get all the official credibility of a company stamp, with a cleaner, more current silhouette.
