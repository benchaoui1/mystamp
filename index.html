Most asked
Can I upload my own design or logo?

Yes — absolutely. Upload your design directly during checkout (PNG, JPG, or PDF). If you prefer, send it via WhatsApp and we'll prepare it for you — at no extra cost. Don't have a logo? Our design team will craft a professional layout for you, completely free.

What types of custom stamps do you offer?

We craft company stamps, signature stamps, date stamps, address stamps, and logo stamps — in round, oval, rectangular, or square shapes. Whether you need a self-inking, pre-inked, or traditional rubber stamp, we've got you covered.

How fast is delivery in the UAE?

Same-day delivery in Dubai with our signature 4-hour express service. For other Emirates, expect next-business-day delivery. All orders include free tracking.

What documents are required to order?

For company stamps, we require your Trade License. This ensures legitimacy and helps us extract your company name in English and Arabic accurately. Your documents are kept strictly confidential.
