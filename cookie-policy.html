Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how GLS PRO (F.Z.E) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website https://mystamp.ae to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and improve performance.

01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and previous actions. They help websites recognize you and provide a personalized experience.

02

Why We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and shopping cart items
  • To improve website performance and load times
  • To track traffic using tools like Google Analytics
03

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though it may impact your experience on our site. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Set preferences for specific sites
04

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back periodically.

05

Contact
